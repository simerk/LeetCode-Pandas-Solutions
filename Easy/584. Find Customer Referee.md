## 🚀 584. Find Customer Referee

### 💡 Idea

We need to find customers who were **not referred by the customer with `id = 2`**.

Since customers without a referee have `NULL` in the `referee_id` column, they should also be included in the result. Therefore, we filter customers whose `referee_id` is **not equal to `2`** or is **`NULL`**.

---

### 💻 Solution

```sql
SELECT name
FROM Customer
WHERE referee_id != 2 OR referee_id IS NULL
ORDER BY id;
```

---

### 🛠️ How It Works

1. Select all customers from the `Customer` table.
2. Filter customers whose `referee_id` is **not equal to `2`**.
3. Include customers whose `referee_id` is `NULL`, indicating they were not referred by anyone.
4. Sort the result by `id`.

---

### ⏱️ Complexity

**Time Complexity:** `O(n)` *(single scan of the `Customer` table)*

**Space Complexity:** `O(1)` *(excluding the output result set)*
