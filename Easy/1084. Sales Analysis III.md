## 🚀 1084. Sales Analysis III

### 💡 Idea

The goal is to find the products that were **sold only during the first quarter of 2019** (from **2019-01-01** to **2019-03-31**).

A product qualifies if:
- It has **at least one sale** during the given date range.
- It has **no sales** before **2019-01-01** or after **2019-03-31**.

There are two common approaches to solve this problem.

---

## 💻 Approach 1: `NOT IN`

```sql
SELECT DISTINCT p.product_id, p.product_name
FROM Product p
INNER JOIN Sales s
ON p.product_id = s.product_id
WHERE p.product_id NOT IN (
    SELECT product_id FROM Sales
    WHERE sale_date < '2019-01-01'
       OR sale_date > '2019-03-31'
);
```

### 🛠️ How It Works

1. Join the `Product` and `Sales` tables using `product_id`.
2. Find all products that have at least one sale **outside** the first quarter of 2019.
3. Exclude those products using `NOT IN`.
4. Use `DISTINCT` to remove duplicate products since a product may have multiple sales.
5. Return the remaining product IDs and names.

---

## 💻 Approach 2: `GROUP BY` + `HAVING`

```sql
SELECT p.product_id, p.product_name
FROM Product p
JOIN Sales s
ON p.product_id = s.product_id
GROUP BY p.product_id, p.product_name
HAVING MIN(s.sale_date) >= '2019-01-01'
   AND MAX(s.sale_date) <= '2019-03-31';
```

### 🛠️ How It Works

1. Join the `Product` and `Sales` tables.
2. Group all sales by product.
3. Find the earliest sale date using `MIN(sale_date)`.
4. Find the latest sale date using `MAX(sale_date)`.
5. Keep only those products whose first and last sale dates both fall within the first quarter of 2019.
6. Return the qualifying product IDs and names.

---

### 📌 Which Approach Should You Use?

- ⭐ **Approach 2 (`GROUP BY` + `HAVING`)** is generally preferred because it scans the sales for each product once and directly checks the date range using `MIN()` and `MAX()`. It is concise and easy to explain.
- ✅ **Approach 1 (`NOT IN`)** is also a good solution and clearly expresses the idea of excluding products with sales outside the required period.

---

### ⏱️ Complexity

| Approach | Time | Space |
|----------|------|-------|
| `NOT IN` | `O(n)` | `O(n)` *(stores excluded product IDs)* |
| `GROUP BY` + `HAVING` | `O(n)` | `O(n)` *(stores grouped products)* |
